The Executive Office of Energy and Environmental Affairs (EEA) invites you to stakeholder sessions to discuss siting and permitting changes under the [2024 Grid Equity Act](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/2024-grid-equity-act-stakeholder-sessions). These sessions will provide an opportunity to review straw proposals, offer feedback, and ask questions. There will be three hybrid sessions and one virtual stakeholder session. [The dates, topics covered in each session, interpretation, and accommodations available are posted here](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/2024-grid-equity-act-stakeholder-sessions) and in the attached notice.
