Between July 2022 and September 2024, Bob Persons wrote a biweekly digest of industry news for Fix the Grid members.  Thank you, Bob! This is the link to the archive for his [Fix the Grid Technical Group Industry News Digest](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1dva_4GwQUv7o0efxof3kiHhhFtJrSpi5hrmwjlx7ALc/edit?usp=sharing).

Bob recommends the following sources for industry news going forward. All of them allow for email subscriptions.

[John Kemp's daily Best in Energy](https://jkempenergy.com/mailing-list/): An interesting big-picture source, particularly from the financial perspective.

[Northeast Energy News](https://energynews.us/northeast/): It draws from a multitude of local and regional sources.

[Utility Dive](https://www.utilitydive.com/):  Reporters have inside connections to FERC and all U.S. regional transmission organizations including ISO-NE. Comprehensive resource.
