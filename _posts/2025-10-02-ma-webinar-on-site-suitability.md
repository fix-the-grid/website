The Massachusetts Executive Office of Energy and Environmental Affairs (EEA) will hold a public webinar on **Thursday, October 9 at 12:00pm** to provide an overview of its [draft Guidance on Site Suitability Assessments for Clean Energy Infrastructure](https://www.mass.gov/doc/draft-guidance-on-site-suitability-assessments-for-clean-energy-infrastructure/download) and the Office of Environmental Justice and Equity’s (OEJE) [draft Standards and Guidelines for Community Benefits Plans and Agreements](https://www.mass.gov/doc/draft-standards-and-guidelines-on-community-benefit-plans-and-agreements/download), and provide an opportunity for public input. [Please register here for the webinar](https://www.zoomgov.com/webinar/register/WN_T2NB7CdnTZWWdj6W2gWgnA#/registration).
