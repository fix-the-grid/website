The Grid Modernization Advisory Council (GMAC) is hosting its second in-person event on **Friday, December 12th** to convene municipal stakeholders and community leaders who are interested in or taking steps to decarbonize and electrify their city or town. Attendees will hear from a panel of speakers from the GMAC and electric distribution companies (EDCs) to learn about the actions Massachusetts is taking to modernize and proactively plan our electric grid. Attendees will engage in facilitated breakout discussions to explore opportunities and challenges of updating our electric grid and identify priority topics for the GMAC’s future work. Additionally, attendees have the option to learn more about distribution system infrastructure through a “virtual” tour of an electric substation. More info and registration **[here](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/the-future-grid-from-a-municipal-lens-new-customer-connections-workshop-tickets-1917591420559?utm-campaign=social&utm-content=attendeeshare&utm-medium=discovery&utm-term=listing&utm-source=cp&aff=ebdsshcopyurl)**.
