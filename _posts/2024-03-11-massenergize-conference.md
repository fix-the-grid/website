[MassEnergize](https://www.massenergize.org/) is hosting a one-day conferencefor climate leaders, community organizers, municipal sustainability officers, and members of climate and sustainability-focused organizations. Anyone working to educate and motivate others to take climate action to reduce GHG emissions in their communities is invited to attend. The conference is **April 3, 2024**at Brandeis University in Waltham.

Speaker, program and registration information is available on the [MassEnergize website](https://www.massenergize.org/).
