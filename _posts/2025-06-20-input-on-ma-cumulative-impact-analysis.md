Conservation Law Foundation, along with The Agency Group, is assisting the Office of Environmental Justice and Equity in convening targeted stakeholder meetings to discuss the draft standards and guidelines for Cumulative Impact Analysis and Community Benefits Plans/Agreements. There will also be a virtual meeting open to the public on June 25, 2025, at 6:00 p.m.
